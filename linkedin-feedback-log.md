## Derived Rules

Rules confirmed from repeated edits — already applied to the skill:

- **Use "entrepreneurs" not "professionals" or "workforce"** — Naga Sai's world and audience is entrepreneurs, not generic professionals.
- **Corporate Connections hashtag: #CorporateConnectionsAPandTS** — one combined hashtag, no spaces or ampersand. Never "#CorporateConnections AP & TS".
- **"community" over "chapter" in public posts** — "chapter" is internal language; "community" is what resonates publicly on LinkedIn.
- **Remove platform-specific references** — don't name specific tools (Slack, WhatsApp, etc.) in posts; keep it generic so it applies broadly.
- **Fewer trailing hashtags** — Naga Sai removes generic end hashtags (#Networking, #Leadership) and keeps only specific ones woven into or at the end of the post.
- **#LeadershipCommunity over #ProfessionalCommunity** — his community is for leaders and entrepreneurs, not generic professionals.
